PPCSexRx

Research prototype for licensed athletic trainers (ATs) supporting adolescents with persistent post-concussion symptoms (PPCS). The tool is intended for AT clinical workflows only, not for direct patient or parent use.

Important: This is not a medical device clearance and not a HIPAA compliance claim. The application is designed to avoid server-side storage of identifiable patient data during routine use. ATs remain responsible for all clinical decisions and for communication with patients and families.

Purpose

PPCSexRx helps ATs apply a published PPCS exercise protocol: eligibility screening, heart-rate prescription guidance, session documentation, and optional analytics when sufficient de-identified session data are available locally in the browser session.

Evidence basis

Recommendations draw on Li (2026), reported as GRADE: LOW certainty evidence. Outputs are conditional suggestions for AT judgment, not definitive treatment directives. See: doi.org/10.17605/OSF.IO/KVUF6

Access

The interactive application requires a modern browser and JavaScript: https://guanglab.org/ppcrx/
